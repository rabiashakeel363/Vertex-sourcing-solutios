Compliance & Certifications

Internationally certified
at every level

All our partner factories comply with globally recognized standards — ensuring ethical production, worker welfare, and supply chain transparency.

Every factory in the Vertex Sourcing Solutions network holds one or more internationally recognized certifications. We only partner with facilities that demonstrate rigorous compliance with global standards for ethical manufacturing, social accountability, and environmental responsibility.

🔒
WRAP
Worldwide Responsible Accredited Production

Ensures lawful, humane, and ethical manufacturing. Covers 12 Principles including compliance with local laws, prohibition of forced labour, and safe working conditions.

🏅
SA8000
Social Accountability International

The leading social certification standard for factories and organizations. Covers child labour, forced labour, health and safety, freedom of association, and fair wages.

📋
ISO 9002
Quality Management System

International standard for quality management systems. Ensures consistent quality output, process control, and continuous improvement across production operations.

🌱
GOTS
Global Organic Textile Standard

The worldwide leading textile processing standard for organic fibres. Defines high-level environmental criteria along the entire organic textile supply chain, backed by social criteria.

🛡️
BSCI
Business Social Compliance Initiative

Supports companies in improving working conditions in their global supply chain. Based on the most important international labour standards including ILO Conventions.

🔗
SEDEX
Supplier Ethical Data Exchange

One of the world's leading ethical trade membership organisations. Provides the Sedex Members Ethical Trade Audit (SMETA) — accepted by major global brands and retailers.

🛂
CTPAT
Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism

US Customs and Border Protection voluntary supply chain security program. Ensures supply chain integrity from manufacturer to US importer — critical for US market access.
