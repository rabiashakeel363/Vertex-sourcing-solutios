Water-Saving Technologies

Precision finishing,
zero waste

By integrating advanced laser and eco-efficient technologies, we now finish a pair of jeans using only a glass of water — compared to the traditional process that consumes up to 70 litres per pair.

01

Ozone Technology

Reduces water and chemical usage while achieving authentic denim finishes. Ozone replaces chemicals in bleaching and fading, cutting wastewater at source while producing consistent, commercial-quality results.

02

E-Flow Technology

Applies treatments using air nano-bubbles, minimizing waste and energy consumption across the finishing process. E-Flow uses up to 95% less water than conventional padding techniques.

03

Laser Technology

Replaces traditional sandblasting entirely — creating precision whiskers, fades, and distress patterns with zero water waste, zero silica dust exposure, and zero chemical use.
