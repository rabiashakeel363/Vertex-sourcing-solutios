Production

Our strength lies in a robust network of partner factories across Pakistan, giving us flexibility and scalability across multiple product categories. Most of our facilities are fully vertical, covering spinning, weaving, dyeing, and garment manufacturing — ensuring consistent quality and shorter lead times.

All our partner factories comply with internationally recognized standards including WRAP, SA8000, and ISO9002, and hold BSCI, SEDEX, CTPAT, and GOTS certifications.

We produce high-quality men's, women's, and children's wear — including circular knits, woven garments, denim, and workwear.

Quality Assurance

Quality is the foundation of everything we do at Vertex Sourcing Solutions. Our process begins at the design and pre-production stages to ensure that quality is built in — not inspected in later.

We maintain full traceability across production lines and post-production checkpoints. Our dedicated independent QA team works closely with every client, assigning a dedicated QC team to each customer to ensure that their specific quality requirements are consistently met and maintained throughout the entire process.

  • Pre-production fabric & trim inspection
  • In-line production checks
  • Final random sampling (AQL standard)
  • Pre-shipment inspection
  • Full traceability documentation

🌍

Logistics

At Vertex Sourcing Solutions, we understand that efficient logistics are essential to seamless global sourcing. Our in-house logistics division manages every detail of transportation, distribution, and fulfillment with precision and reliability.

Through our network of trusted shipping and freight partners, we provide flexible shipping options, competitive rates, and full DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) services to simplify import processes for our clients.

We emphasize transparency and communication, providing real-time tracking, proactive updates, and responsive customer support to ensure on-time, hassle-free deliveries every time.
